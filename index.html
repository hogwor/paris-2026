03

Where to Stay

Airbnb shortlist · 3–4 bedrooms for 8

The idea: stay central for sightseeing, take the train to the game. The RER B line links central Paris to the stadium — near Saint-Michel or Châtelet means a one-train ride on game day.
Top pick · $$

Latin Quarter (5th)

✓

Direct RER B to the stadium from Saint-Michel + the best first-trip sightseeing area. Safe, lively, great cheap eats for a group. Search near the Sorbonne/Panthéon; avoid the Rue de la Huchette tourist strip.

$$$

Le Marais (3rd/4th)

✓

Most charming, classic Paris. Steps from Notre-Dame. One metro hop to Châtelet for the RER B. Ask for an elevator ("ascenseur").

$$$

Châtelet / Les Halles (1st/2nd)

✓

Dead center, direct RER B, walk to the Louvre & Marais. Pick a quiet side street off the big boulevards.

$$$$

Saint-Germain (6th)

✓

Elegant & calm, near the Latin Quarter's RER B access. Priciest; fewer cheap group meals.

Airbnb filters: 3–4 real bedrooms (check bed setup) · ~10-min walk to Saint-Michel or Châtelet · elevator · registered listing (has a registration number) · quiet/courtyard unit · lots of recent reviews.
